Turret Defense

Turret Defense is a tower defense game. You must help the turrets from the enemies from taking over their territory, by spawning turrets on the battle field.

As you defeat more and more enemies, the levels get harder. You must help the turrets drive these pesky enemies out of your territory. Spawn turrets, and upgrade them to defend, and attack. The enemies attack in groups of waves. As the level goes further, the harder the waves are. So, you must use your money wisely to upgrade and spawn turrets.
