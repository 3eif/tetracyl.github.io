About Us

We are student developers that make games in our free time with Unity C#. One of our very first games is Kravity that we made in a game jam under 48 hours. We have also made Turret Defense, a tower defense game based on a tutorial that is available on Mac, Windows, Linux, and Android. We started making games with Scratch, and continued to make games with Greenfoot and Java. Now we have moved on to Unity Engine.
